Connect with like-minded Westside High School students interested in computer programming.

What We Do

We explore software developement and get hands on, developing our own applications. At the HISD Together with Tech Conference held in the spring, members can present apps they developed.

Learn and compete in programming competitions. We're involved in the Computer Science University Interscholastic League (UIL) and in the spring, HP CodeWars. Regardless of skill, our focus with these competitions and HP CodeWars is for you to learn and experience the atmosphere.

Learn more

Attend our introductory and welcome meeting held on September 10 during Lunch in S133.
If you can't attend, please go to S113 for information.

Our members are a diverse set of people, each with different skills and experience. Everyone is welcoming, and as a small club, we have the advantage of bonding and getting to know each other well.
